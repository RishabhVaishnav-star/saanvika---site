Saanvika (Pooja Singh)

Indian Actress | Panchayat Fame

Early Life and Education

Saanvika was born in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh on 8 January 1990. She holds a BTech degree in Electronics and Communication. She currently resides in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Career

Saanvika made her debut in the web series Panchayat as Rinki Dubey. She also played supporting roles in Lakhan Leela Bhargava and Hajamat in 2023.

Awards

She won the Popular Choice Actor at the Global Excellence Award in 2024 for her role in Panchayat.
